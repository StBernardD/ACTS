What is ACTS?

ACTS is an interfaith ministry dedicated to the needs of our neighbors in Bulloch County. ACTS provides Bulloch County residents one-time emergency financial assistance. Monies are provided by donations to ACTS from local churches, United Way allocations, grants and personal donations.

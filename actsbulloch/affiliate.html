HOW CAN OUR CHURCH HELP?

  1. Financial Support
    1. Regular monthly contributions. Some churches contribute monthly to ACTS with monies budgeted by their churches. Other churches have special monthly collections to benefit ACTS. Still others contribute periodically as they can.
    2. Memorial contributions. Honor an individual, say thank you for a special favor or a job well done, or make a donation in the name of a deceased friend or family member. Memorial cards with the ACTS logo will be sent to the honored individual or to the deceasedâ€™s family.
    3. Individual contributions.
    4. Grants from United Way, businesses and service organizations.
    5. Fundraisers to benefit ACTS.
    6. In kind donations of office supplies.
  2. Encourage Volunteers. ACTS needs volunteers to work both at ACTS and at Worn Threads, the thrift store.
    1. At ACTS, volunteers greet applicants and hand out applications, make copies, interview, enter interview data on off days, and answer telephones.
    2. Worn Threads volunteers operate the store, greet customers, provide help in finding desired items and take money for sales. Volunteers also sort donations and restock store.

Mission Statement

To provide a means of cooperation and communication between churches, businesses, civic organizations, government agencies, schools and individuals in meeting charitable, benevolent and educational community needs.

Mission Goals

  • To help those in need without regard to race, religion, nationality or ethnic background.
  • To engage the community in offering “neighbor-to- neighbor” assistance.
  • To have one central location, negating the necessity of referring clients to other ministries.
  • To have a network of volunteers of various skills and talents available to interview clients & to meet other emergencies as they arise.
  • To identify and work with agencies and other resources available to the needy or those in crisis situations.
  • To maximize resources & prevent duplication of services.
  • To inform the public of community members’ needs.
  • To promote harmony and understanding in citizens throughout Bulloch County.

Advantages of Affiliating with ACTS

  1. Referral to ACTS of persons seeking financial assistance; freedom from interruptions of regular duties.
  2. Screening is offsite, so church building is more secure.
  3. Centralized clearinghouse for emergency financial aid. Computerized recordkeeping to help identify those who truly need assistance, and those who are abusing the system.
  4. Screening and verification of applicant’s situation.
  5. Knowledge of other services available to applicant through a close working relationship with area service agencies.
  6. Recordkeeping, filing, check writing and delivery done by ACTS.
